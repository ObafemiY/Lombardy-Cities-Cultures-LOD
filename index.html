City Highlights

Milan

Known for Milan Fashion Week, attracting designers and fashion enthusiasts from around the world.

Bergamo

Celebrates Sant’Alessandro Feast with parades and historical re-enactments in honor of the city’s patron saint.

Mantua

Hosts the Festivaletteratura, a celebrated literary festival that draws writers and readers from all over.
