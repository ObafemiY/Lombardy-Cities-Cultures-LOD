City Highlights

Milan

Known for Milan Fashion Week, attracting designers and fashion enthusiasts from around the world.

Bergamo

Celebrates Sant’Alessandro Feast with parades and historical re-enactments in honor of the city’s patron saint.

Brescia

Known as the 'most beautiful race in the world,' this historical car race attracts vintage car enthusiasts from around the globe.

Varese

Known for the Sacro Monte di Varese, A pilgrimage route and UNESCO site.

Como

Sagra di San Giovanni — Held on Lake Como, this festival celebrates St. John with a boat parade, fireworks, and a historical re-enactment.
